Shamir's Secret Sharing

A cryptographic algorithm to securely share and reconstruct secrets

What is Shamir's Secret Sharing?

Shamir's Secret Sharing is a cryptographic method that allows you to divide a secret into multiple parts (shares), where:

  • Each individual share reveals nothing about the original secret
  • Only by combining a minimum number of shares (threshold) can the secret be reconstructed
  • Even with one share less than the threshold, the secret remains completely protected

This provides both security and fault tolerance for sensitive information.
